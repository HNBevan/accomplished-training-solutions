Professional Specialist Training

Accomplished Training Solutions delivers specialist workplace training courses across the whole of the UK. Our specialist courses address some of the most important areas of modern workplace compliance, culture and wellbeing — from disability awareness and equality legislation to mental health first aid. All training is delivered on-site at your premises by experienced specialist trainers.

Our specialist courses are designed to give your team the knowledge and confidence to create a more inclusive, supportive and legally compliant workplace environment. Certificates are issued on completion of all courses.

Disability Discrimination Awareness

Approx 3.5 Hours Certificate Provided

This course gives delegates a thorough understanding of disability discrimination legislation, the barriers faced by disabled people, and how to communicate confidently and competently with disabled colleagues and visitors. Practical and engaging, it helps businesses meet their legal obligations while building a genuinely inclusive workplace culture.

Course Content

Legal Aspects

  • The Disability Discrimination Act 1995
  • Provision of goods and services
  • Employment legislation and obligations
  • Facilities and reasonable adjustments
  • Understanding disabilities
  • Removal of barriers for disabled visitors and disabled workers
  • Disability etiquette in the workplace

Impairment & Disability

  • Mobility impairments
  • Learning difficulties
  • Mental health issues
  • Visual impairments
  • Hearing impairments
  • Hidden impairments and long-term health conditions
  • Physical, informational/communication, and attitudinal barriers
  • Confidence and competence in communicating with disabled people

Models of Disability

  • The Medical Model vs The Social Model of disability

Who Should Attend?

Essential for HR managers, team leaders, customer-facing staff, facilities managers, and any employee whose role involves interacting with or managing disabled colleagues or customers. Suitable for all business sectors.

Mental Health First Aid

Approx 3.5 Hours Certificate Provided

This course introduces delegates to the role of a mental health first aider, equipping them with practical tools to recognise signs of mental health challenges in the workplace and provide early, compassionate support. Reducing stigma and knowing how to respond can make a real difference to colleagues in crisis.

Course Content

  • Introduction to MHFA - Defining the role of a mental health first aider, reducing stigma, and understanding the impact of mental health challenges
  • Mental Health Problems & Crises - Recognising signs and symptoms of depression, anxiety, psychosis, and substance use
  • The ALGEE Action Plan - A structured approach: Assess for risk, Listen non-judgementally, Give reassurance, Encourage professional help, Encourage self-help
  • Crisis First Aid - Specific protocols for suicide risk, panic attacks, non-suicidal self-injury, and acute psychotic episodes
  • Support & Resources - Identifying professional help, local resources, and self-care strategies for the helper

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for line managers, HR professionals, team leaders, wellbeing champions, and any employee who wants to be better equipped to support a colleague experiencing a mental health challenge. No prior mental health knowledge is required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are your specialist courses suitable for?

Our specialist courses are designed for employers, managers, HR professionals, and team leaders across all industries. Disability Discrimination Awareness is ideal for anyone responsible for staff management or customer facing roles, while Mental Health First Aid is relevant to any workplace wanting to support employee wellbeing and meet duty of care obligations.

Are these courses legally required?

While not always a strict legal requirement, both courses help businesses comply with the Equality Act 2010 and Health & Safety at Work Act 1974. Disability awareness training demonstrates a commitment to inclusive practice, and Mental Health First Aid training supports your duty of care to employees under current HSE guidance. Many organisations now treat these as essential rather than optional.

Can these courses be tailored to our organisation?

Yes, all specialist training is delivered on-site and can be adapted to reflect your specific workplace environment, policies, and team needs. We work with you before the session to understand your context so the training is as relevant and impactful as possible. Group bookings are available with discounts for larger teams.
