Professional First Aid Training

Accomplished Training Solutions delivers comprehensive first aid training courses across the whole of the UK. All our first aid courses are HSE compliant and meet current UK workplace legislation requirements. We bring expert training directly to your workplace, saving time and reducing costs.

Our experienced trainers have real-world experience and deliver practical, engaging courses that ensure your team is confident and competent in emergency first aid situations. Certificates are issued on the same day of training completion.

Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW)

1 Day Valid for 3 Years

The Emergency First Aid at Work (EFAW) course is ideal for low-risk workplaces and provides essential first aid skills. This one-day HSE-approved course covers the fundamentals of emergency first aid response.

Course Content

  • Understanding roles and responsibilities of a first aider
  • Assessing an incident and managing casualties
  • CPR and use of defibrillators (AED)
  • Managing an unresponsive casualty
  • Treating wounds and bleeding
  • Minor injuries including burns and scalds
  • Shock management

Who Should Attend?

This course is suitable for employees in lower-risk environments, those requiring basic first aid knowledge, and businesses with smaller teams. It's also ideal for refresher training or those wanting fundamental first aid skills.

First Aid at Work (FAW) - 3 Day

3 Days Valid for 3 Years

The comprehensive First Aid at Work (FAW) course is a three-day HSE-approved qualification for workplaces. This in-depth course provides extensive knowledge and practical skills to deal with a wide range of first aid emergencies.

Course Content

  • Understanding roles, responsibilities and health & safety legislation
  • Assessing incidents and managing emergency situations
  • CPR and automated external defibrillator (AED) use
  • Managing unresponsive casualties and choking
  • Treating wounds, bleeding and shock
  • Minor injuries including burns, scalds and eye injuries
  • Fractures, dislocations and spinal injuries
  • Chest injuries and medical emergencies
  • Anaphylaxis, heart attacks, strokes, asthma and diabetes
  • Poisoning and electrical injuries
  • Recording and reporting accidents

Who Should Attend?

This course is essential for designated workplace first aiders, employees in higher-risk environments (construction, manufacturing, warehouses), and businesses where the HSE requires qualified first aiders. Ideal for 5+ employees or specific risk assessments.

First Aid at Work Requalification

2 Days Valid for 3 Years

The First Aid at Work Requalification course is designed for individuals who hold a current or recently expired FAW certificate (within 28 days). This two-day refresher updates your first aid knowledge and skills to maintain your qualification.

Course Content

  • Review of first aider roles and responsibilities
  • Updated emergency response procedures
  • CPR and AED refresher training
  • Treating casualties with various injuries and conditions
  • Latest first aid techniques and protocols
  • Practical scenario-based assessments

Who Should Attend?

This course is for anyone who currently holds a First Aid at Work certificate that is due to expire or has expired within the last 28 days. If your certificate has been expired for more than 28 days, you'll need to complete the full 3-day FAW course.

Paediatric First Aid

2 Days Valid for 3 Years

Our Paediatric First Aid course is specifically designed for those working with babies and children. This comprehensive two-day course is Ofsted compliant and meets EYFS requirements for nurseries, schools, and childcare providers.

Course Content

  • Roles and responsibilities when caring for children
  • Infant and child CPR techniques
  • Managing choking in babies and children
  • Dealing with an unresponsive child or infant
  • Treating wounds and bleeding in children
  • Burns, scalds and electrical injuries
  • Common childhood illnesses and allergies
  • Febrile convulsions and meningitis recognition
  • Head and spinal injuries
  • Asthma and anaphylaxis in children
  • Recording and reporting procedures

Who Should Attend?

Essential for nursery staff, childminders, teachers, teaching assistants, sports coaches, and anyone working with children in a professional capacity. This course is mandatory for Ofsted registered childcare providers.

Basic First Aid Awareness

Half Day Certificate Provided

Our Basic First Aid Awareness course provides fundamental first aid knowledge in a condensed half-day format. Perfect for general staff awareness and those requiring an introduction to first aid principles.

Course Content

  • Introduction to first aid principles
  • Emergency response basics
  • CPR awareness
  • Dealing with common injuries
  • When to call for help
  • Basic wound care

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for all employees who want basic first aid awareness, companies providing general health and safety training, and those who want to support designated first aiders. Not a regulated qualification but provides valuable awareness training.
