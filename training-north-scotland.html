Frequently Asked Questions

Are there specific cold weather or winter safety training modules required in the North and Scotland?

Yes. Mandatory winter training covers topics like slips and falls on ice, safe use of de-icing agents, maintaining adequate vehicle breakdown kits, and procedures for accessing sites during extreme weather conditions.

Does the Scottish Government's regulatory framework affect our standard H&S training content?

While the core legal duties remain the same (HSE-enforced), training may highlight areas where Scottish regulations provide specific guidance (e.g., zero-emission zones). All policies confirm adherence to the HSE (GB) standards regardless of location.

What training is provided regarding work in or near water (e.g., ports, waterways in the North)?

For any employee whose duties bring them near docks, rivers, or large bodies of water, mandatory Water Safety Awareness Training and location-specific training on the use of throw lines or life jackets is required.
