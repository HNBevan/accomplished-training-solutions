Professional Health & Safety Training

Accomplished Training Solutions delivers comprehensive health and safety training courses across the whole of the UK. All our health and safety courses meet current UK workplace legislation requirements including the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974. We bring expert training directly to your workplace, saving time and reducing costs.

Our experienced trainers provide practical, engaging courses that ensure your team understands their legal responsibilities, workplace hazards, and how to create a safer working environment. Certificates are issued on the same day of training completion.

Manual Handling Training

Half Day Valid for 3 Years

Our Manual Handling Training course provides essential knowledge and practical techniques to reduce the risk of injury when lifting, carrying, pushing, and pulling loads. This course meets the requirements of the Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992.

Course Content

  • Understanding manual handling legislation and regulations
  • Common manual handling injuries and their causes
  • Anatomy of the spine and musculoskeletal system
  • Risk assessment for manual handling tasks
  • Safe lifting techniques and practical exercises
  • Pushing, pulling, and carrying techniques
  • Use of mechanical aids and equipment
  • Team handling and communication
  • Recognizing and reporting hazards

Who Should Attend?

Essential for warehouse staff, delivery drivers, healthcare workers, retail employees, maintenance staff, and anyone who regularly handles loads as part of their role. Suitable for all industries where manual handling is required.

Working at Heights Training

1 Day Valid for 3 Years

Our Working at Heights course provides comprehensive training on working safely at height in compliance with the Work at Height Regulations 2005. This course covers hazard identification, risk assessment, and safe working practices.

Course Content

  • Work at Height Regulations 2005 overview
  • Legal duties and responsibilities
  • Identifying hazards when working at height
  • Risk assessment and hierarchy of control
  • Selection and inspection of equipment (ladders, scaffolds, towers)
  • Safe use of ladders and stepladders
  • Mobile elevated work platforms (MEWPs) awareness
  • Fall prevention and fall protection systems
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) for height work
  • Emergency procedures and rescue planning
  • Safe access and egress

Who Should Attend?

Critical for construction workers, maintenance technicians, facilities managers, roofers, window cleaners, electricians, and anyone who works at height. Essential for supervisors and managers responsible for height work activities.

IOSH Managing Safely

3 Days Valid for 3 Years

IOSH Managing Safely is the internationally recognized health and safety qualification for managers and supervisors. This comprehensive three-day course covers all aspects of workplace health and safety management.

Course Content

  • Introducing managing safely
  • Assessing risks in the workplace
  • Controlling risks and developing safe systems of work
  • Understanding responsibilities and legal frameworks
  • Understanding hazards and their consequences
  • Investigating incidents and learning from failures
  • Measuring performance and monitoring systems
  • Protecting the environment
  • Risk assessment practical exercises
  • Action planning for workplace improvements
  • Assessment (written test and practical risk assessment)

Who Should Attend?

Essential for managers, supervisors, team leaders, and anyone with health and safety responsibilities. Suitable for all industries and sectors. Provides internationally recognized IOSH certification upon successful completion.

Risk Assessment Training

1 Day Certificate Provided

Our Risk Assessment Training course provides the knowledge and skills to identify workplace hazards and conduct effective risk assessments. This practical course meets legal requirements under the Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999.

Course Content

  • Health and safety legislation and requirements
  • Understanding hazards, risks, and controls
  • The five steps of risk assessment
  • Identifying workplace hazards
  • Determining who might be harmed and how
  • Evaluating risks and control measures
  • Recording findings and creating action plans
  • Implementing control measures hierarchy
  • Review and monitoring of assessments
  • Specific risk assessments (manual handling, COSHH, DSE)
  • Practical risk assessment exercises

Who Should Attend?

Essential for health and safety officers, managers, supervisors, and anyone responsible for conducting workplace risk assessments. Suitable for all industries requiring documented risk assessment procedures.

COSHH Awareness Training

Half Day Certificate Provided

Our COSHH (Control of Substances Hazardous to Health) Awareness course provides essential training on working safely with hazardous substances. This course meets the requirements of the COSHH Regulations 2002.

Course Content

  • Introduction to COSHH Regulations 2002
  • Understanding hazardous substances in the workplace
  • Health effects of exposure to hazardous substances
  • Routes of entry into the body
  • Understanding safety data sheets (SDS)
  • Hazard symbols and labeling (CLP/GHS)
  • COSHH risk assessment principles
  • Control measures and hierarchy of control
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) for hazardous substances
  • Safe storage, handling, and disposal
  • Emergency procedures and spill response
  • Health surveillance and monitoring

Who Should Attend?

Essential for employees working with cleaning chemicals, solvents, paints, adhesives, or any hazardous substances. Suitable for manufacturing, laboratories, cleaning staff, maintenance teams, and healthcare workers.
