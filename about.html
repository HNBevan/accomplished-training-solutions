Fully Compliant, Legally Certified

HSE Compliance

All our training courses are fully compliant with Health and Safety Executive (HSE) requirements and current UK legislation. We stay up-to-date with every regulatory change to ensure your business meets its legal obligations.

Recognized Certifications

Our certifications are recognized nationwide and accepted by insurers, regulatory bodies, and during workplace inspections. Certificates are issued on the day of training and are valid for three years.

Legal Peace of Mind

With Accomplished Training Solutions, you can demonstrate due diligence to regulatory authorities, reduce liability exposure, and ensure complete compliance with UK health and safety legislation including:

  • Health and Safety at Work Act 1974
  • Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999
  • Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005
  • Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992
  • Food Safety Act 1990
