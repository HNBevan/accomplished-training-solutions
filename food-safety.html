Professional Food Safety Training

Accomplished Training Solutions delivers comprehensive food safety and hygiene training courses across the whole of the UK. All our food safety courses are nationally accredited and meet UK food hygiene legislation requirements. We bring expert training directly to your workplace, saving time and reducing costs.

Our experienced trainers provide practical, engaging courses that ensure your team understands food safety principles, hazard control, and their legal responsibilities. Certificates are issued on the same day of training completion, ensuring immediate compliance.

Level 1 Food Safety

Half Day Valid for 3 Years

Our Level 1 Food Safety course provides an introduction to food hygiene for those working in low-risk food handling environments. This foundational course covers essential food safety principles and is ideal for new starters in the food industry.

Course Content

  • Introduction to food safety and hygiene
  • Understanding food contamination and bacteria
  • Personal hygiene practices
  • Food storage and temperature control basics
  • Cleaning and disinfection principles
  • Pest awareness and prevention
  • Legal responsibilities in food safety
  • Food allergen awareness

Who Should Attend?

Ideal for staff in low-risk food handling roles, porters, bar staff, waiting staff, room service attendants, and those new to the food industry requiring basic food safety knowledge.

Level 2 Food Hygiene

1 Day Valid for 3 Years

Our Level 2 Food Hygiene course is the industry standard qualification for anyone handling food. This comprehensive one-day course covers all aspects of food safety and is a legal requirement for most food handlers in the UK.

Course Content

  • Food safety legislation and enforcement
  • Microbiological hazards and food poisoning bacteria
  • Bacterial contamination and multiplication
  • High-risk and low-risk foods
  • Personal hygiene and protective clothing
  • Temperature control and monitoring
  • Food storage, preparation and cooking
  • Cross-contamination prevention
  • Cleaning, disinfection and sanitization
  • Pest control and prevention
  • Food premises design and maintenance
  • Food allergen management and labeling
  • HACCP awareness

Who Should Attend?

Essential for chefs, cooks, kitchen assistants, catering staff, food production workers, retail food handlers, and anyone who handles, prepares or serves food in a professional capacity.

Allergen Awareness Training

2-3 Hours Certificate Provided

Our Allergen Awareness course provides essential knowledge about food allergens and legal requirements under Natasha's Law. This focused training ensures your team can identify allergens, prevent cross-contamination, and provide accurate allergen information to customers.

Course Content

  • Understanding food allergies and intolerances
  • The 14 major food allergens
  • Legal requirements and Natasha's Law
  • Allergen labeling and information requirements
  • Reading and interpreting food labels
  • Preventing allergen cross-contamination
  • Menu planning and allergen documentation
  • Safe food preparation for allergen sufferers
  • Communicating allergen information to customers
  • Responding to allergen emergencies
  • Record keeping and traceability

Who Should Attend?

Essential for all food handlers, kitchen staff, waiting staff, bar staff, catering managers, and anyone involved in food preparation, service or customer communication in restaurants, cafes, hotels, and catering businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which food safety courses do you offer?

We offer Level 1 Food Safety (Half Day) for staff with minimal food handling responsibilities, Level 2 Food Safety (1 Day) for all food handlers, the industry standard qualification and Allergen Awareness (2–3 Hours) covering the 14 major allergens and legal requirements. All courses are delivered on-site at your premises.

Who needs food safety training?

Anyone who handles, prepares, or serves food in a professional setting should hold a food safety certificate. Level 1 is suitable for front-of-house or low contact roles, while Level 2 is the standard requirement for kitchen staff, chefs, and food handlers. Allergen Awareness is essential for any business serving food to the public under Natasha's Law.

How long are food safety certificates valid?

Food safety certificates are generally valid for 3 years, after which a refresher course is recommended to stay compliant. We can help schedule renewal training before your current certificates expire.
