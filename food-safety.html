Professional Food Safety Training

Accomplished Training Solutions delivers comprehensive food safety and hygiene training courses across the whole of the UK. All our food safety courses are nationally accredited and meet UK food hygiene legislation requirements. We bring expert training directly to your workplace, saving time and reducing costs.

Our experienced trainers provide practical, engaging courses that ensure your team understands food safety principles, hazard control, and their legal responsibilities. Certificates are issued on the same day of training completion, ensuring immediate compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which level of Food Safety certification is mandatory for my role?

Employees who directly handle food (e.g., chefs, servers) are required to obtain Level 2 Food Hygiene certification. Staff in supervisory or management roles require the more advanced Level 3 certification.

How long is my Food Safety Certificate valid for, and when must I renew it?

Food Safety certificates typically have a validity period of three years from the date of issue. You must complete a full refresher training course before your current certificate expires to maintain compliance.

What are the prerequisites for attending the advanced Level 3 Food Safety course?

To enroll in the Level 3 (Supervisory) course, you must have already successfully completed and hold a valid Level 2 Food Hygiene certificate. This ensures you have the foundational knowledge required for advanced topics.
